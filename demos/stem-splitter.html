AI Stem Splitter

Upload a song and split it into separate stems — vocals, drums, bass, and more — then download each as its own audio file. The separation runs on Demucs, a pretrained deep-learning source-separation model, with a built-in assistant that suggests which stems to generate for your goal (karaoke, remix, instrument practice, transcription).

Open full app ↗ Related code ↗

Hosted free on Hugging Face Spaces (CPU). The demo may take a moment to wake if it has been idle, and separating a full song runs in roughly 1–3 minutes. If the embed doesn't load, open it in a new tab.
